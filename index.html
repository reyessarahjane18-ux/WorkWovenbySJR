About Me

Hi, I’m Sarah Jane Reyes — a dedicated Loan Processing Specialist with 6+ years of experience across the Australian broker channel and the US loan markets. I specialize in making loan files move seamlessly from application to approval, ensuring accuracy, compliance, and efficiency at every step.

Beyond loan processing, I also bring expertise in data entry, verification, and customer service. Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of working with some of the biggest names in the industry such as Wells Fargo, AT&T, Doordash, and Resolve Finance.

My goal is simple: to save you time, minimize stress, and help your business grow with reliable back-end support. When I’m not managing files, you’ll find me exploring new ways to improve workflows, staying updated on financial guidelines, and delivering excellent client experiences.
